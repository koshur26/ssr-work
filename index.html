About the Advocate

A Counsel of Integrity & Diligence

Advocate Syed Sibtain Razvi & Associates is a well-regarded law firm in Srinagar, enrolled with the Bar Council of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh. As one of the trusted advocates in Srinagar, he appears regularly before the Hon'ble High Court of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh, before the Subordinate Courts, and various Tribunals across the region.

With a keen legal acumen and a meticulous approach to every brief, our legal services in Srinagar span civil disputes, criminal defence, family law, property matters, corporate advisory, arbitration, and constitutional petitions — making us one of the more versatile lawyers in Srinagar for clients across Kashmir.

As an advocate practising in Jammu & Kashmir, the firm's approach is founded on confidentiality, thoroughness, and zealous advocacy — the same principles that guide the best advocates in Kashmir — ensuring every client receives the highest quality of legal representation and counsel.
