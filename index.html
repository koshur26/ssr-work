About the Advocate

A Counsel of Integrity & Diligence

Advocate Syed Sibtain Razvi is a practising advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh. He appears regularly before the Hon'ble High Court of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh at Srinagar, before the Subordinate Courts, and various Tribunals across Jammu & Kashmir.

With a keen legal acumen and a meticulous approach to every brief, he has successfully represented clients in matters spanning civil disputes, criminal defence, family law, property matters, and constitutional petitions.

His practice is founded on the principles of confidentiality, thoroughness, and zealous advocacy — ensuring that every client receives the highest quality of legal representation and counsel.
